Transparency

One of the remarkable features of DeFi protocols is their commitment to transparency.

The Vestivado Ecosystem

Vestivado Finance embodies this principle by aiming to establish a decentralized, autonomous, and self-learning asset management protocol within the emerging Web3 ecosystem. Our vision is to make crypto and venture investing accessible, straightforward, and inclusive. The recent collapses of centralized entities like FTX and Three Arrows underscore the need for a 100% transparent capital fund, a need that Vestivado Finance fulfills.

Community Ownership and Transparency

Vestivado Finance proudly operates as a community-owned and monitored platform. All smart contracts, treasury addresses, and multi-signature wallets are publicly accessible in our official documents, providing users with complete insight into the underlying assets of the protocol. Upon the protocol's launch, Vestivado Finance will automatically generate asset reports to further enhance transparency.

Stage 1 - Structuring the Fund

Our protocol introduces innovative features, including the ability to bond various crypto assets into Protocol Organized Assets (POA) for VFD tokens at discounted rates. This not only stabilizes the protocol but also unlocks yield opportunities for VFD token holders. Key highlights include:

  1. Bond-in: Earn a performance-based VFD bonus when bonding-in.
  2. Staking Function: Stake VFD and LP tokens to generate yields.
  3. Automated Buy-back Function: Automatic buybacks are triggered when the VFD token's price experiences a decline exceeding 20%.

Stage 2 - Implementing a Low-Slippage Swap Function

This stage introduces an experience akin to a decentralized exchange, with a primary focus on facilitating token swaps on Aptos and Sui with minimal slippage. It addresses liquidity and slippage concerns through:

  1. Streamlining token swaps for efficiency
  2. The Swap Function, Streamlining token swaps for efficiency.
  3. Automated Arbitrage, Smart contract-based arbitrage

Main Focus

  • The utilization of Vestivado funds to ensure stablecoin liquidity on Aptos and Sui.
  • Partial automation of arbitrage, with discretionary intervention in turbulent market conditions.
  • The implementation of the invariant swap on Aptos & Sui to facilitate highly efficient stablecoin trades.

Stage 3 - Bridging and Asset Utilization

In this phase, Vestivado Finance's asset portfolio encompasses a diverse array of crypto assets, including stablecoins, pegged assets, major tokens, and early-stage projects. Stage 3 is dedicated to integrating with other chains, such as ETH and EVM-compatible chains, with the objective of creating an advanced AMM that enables efficient trading and reduces risks across various chains.

Key highlights include:

  • The efficient utilization of liquidity, enabling the pool to adapt to the region with the highest liquidity without incurring losses.
  • The inclusion of tokens such as DAI, USDC, USDT, ETH, WBTC, and more.
